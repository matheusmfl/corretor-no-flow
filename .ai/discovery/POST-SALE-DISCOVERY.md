# Post-Sale Discovery

Post-sale is a future product phase. Do not mix it into Pre-Sale V1 implementation tasks.

## Product Intent

Help brokers retain insured clients after the sale by giving the client a useful portal and giving the brokerage a faster emergency/support workflow.

## Candidate Capabilities

- Insured portal accessed by phone, without email/password.
- Broker uploads policy/proposal and links it to the quote that generated the sale.
- QR code from proposal/PDF to client portal.
- Contracted insurance list.
- Policy validity and renewal countdown.
- Payment schedule and due dates.
- Useful phone numbers and assistance contacts.
- Insured card with policy number and emergency contacts.
- Emergency request button.
- Broker assignment: one broker marks "I will handle this".
- Notification to other brokers when someone takes ownership.
- AI triage if no broker responds quickly.

## Risks To Resolve Later

- Authentication by phone only needs fraud and privacy review.
- Emergency messaging cannot imply guaranteed rescue.
- AI guidance must avoid unsafe legal, medical, or emergency advice.
- WhatsApp notification flow needs provider and consent decisions.
- Broker assignment needs real-time or near-real-time status handling.

## Not V1

This discovery should not create implementation tasks until Pre-Sale V1 is stronger.

# Prospecting Discovery

Prospecting is a future phase after the quote and renewal loop is working.

## Candidate Capabilities

- Broker content library.
- AI-assisted support material generation.
- Insurance news monitoring.
- Email marketing templates.
- Lead list and campaign sending.
- Broker landing page.
- Linktree-style profile.
- Dynamic lead capture forms for quote requests.

## Product Principle

Prospecting should connect to the broker's real lead and renewal pipeline. Avoid building generic marketing tools before the CRM/opportunity model exists.
